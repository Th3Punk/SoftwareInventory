# Authentication

## Logging In

1. Navigate to the login page
2. Enter your username and password
3. Click "Login"

On successful login, you will be redirected to the main application.

## First Login

If this is your first time logging in (or your password was reset by
an administrator), you will be asked to change your password before
you can access the application.

## Password Requirements

Your password must meet the following criteria:

- At least 12 characters long
- Contains at least one uppercase letter
- Contains at least one digit
- Contains at least one special character (e.g., `!`, `@`, `#`)

## Changing Your Password

1. Go to your profile or use the change password option
2. Enter your current password
3. Enter your new password (must meet the requirements above)
4. Confirm your new password
5. Click "Change Password"

## Account Lockout

If you enter the wrong password too many times, your account will be
temporarily locked. Wait a few minutes and try again. If the issue
persists, contact your administrator.

## Logging Out

Click "Logout" in the application menu. Your session will be ended
and you will need to log in again to access the application.
